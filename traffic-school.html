Common Questions

Who is eligible for traffic school?expand_more

In California, you may be eligible for traffic school if you have a valid driver's license, the ticket was for a non-commercial vehicle, and you haven't attended traffic school in the past 18 months.

How long do I have to complete traffic school?expand_more

You must complete traffic school by the deadline printed on your courtesy notice, which is typically the same as your court appearance date. Contact us early to avoid missing the deadline.

Does traffic school clear the ticket from my record?expand_more

Traffic school masks the violation from your insurance record so your insurance company won't see it. The ticket still appears on your DMV record but insurance companies can't use it to raise your rates.
