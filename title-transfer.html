Common Questions

How long does a title transfer take?expand_more

Processing at our office takes about 15-20 minutes. The new title is then mailed by the CA DMV within 4-6 weeks.

What if the seller is out of state?expand_more

We handle out-of-state title transfers. The process may require additional documentation. Contact us and we'll guide you through exactly what's needed.

Do I need to pay use tax?expand_more

California use tax is typically required on private-party vehicle purchases. We'll calculate your exact tax owed based on the purchase price and county.
