What is an FLSP?

A Field Licensing Service Provider (FLSP) is a business authorized by the California DMV to provide registration and titling services directly to the public. As an FLSP, EZ Auto License is held to strict state standards and can process transactions that normally require a DMV office visit.

Important Note

EZ Auto License is not a branch of the California DMV. We are a licensed private company authorized to perform DMV services on behalf of our customers.
