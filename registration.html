Common Questions

How long does registration renewal take? expand_more

Most renewals are completed in under 5 minutes. After payment, you'll receive a digital e-sticker confirmation immediately by email. Physical tags are mailed and arrive within 3-5 business days.

Do I need a smog check to renew? expand_more

Most vehicles over 4 years old require a smog check every two years. When you enter your plate, we'll instantly tell you if your vehicle needs a smog test before renewal can be completed.

Is EZ Auto License an official DMV partner? expand_more

Yes. EZ Auto License is a CA DMV authorized Field Licensing Service Provider (FLSP). We are not a branch of the CA DMV, but we are officially licensed to process registrations directly with the state.

What if my registration is expired? expand_more

We can process expired registrations. Late fees may apply depending on how long the registration has been expired. Enter your plate to see your exact fees including any penalties.
